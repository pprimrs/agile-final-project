User stories follow the format:  
**As a [role], I want [functionality], so that [business value].**

---

### **PB-01 Create Product**
As an **admin**, I want to **create a product in the catalog**, so that **I can manage items available to customers**.

**Acceptance Criteria**
- Admin can fill in product name, price, category, and image.
- Product appears in the catalog after saving.
- Error message shows if required fields are empty.

---

### **PB-02 Update Product**
As an **admin**, I want to **update a product**, so that **information stays correct and up-to-date**.

**Acceptance Criteria**
- Editable fields: name, price, photo, description.
- Product list refreshes after update.

---

### **PB-03 Delete Product**
As an **admin**, I want to **delete a product**, so that **outdated items are removed**.

**Acceptance Criteria**
- Confirmation popup before deletion.
- Product disappears after confirming.

---

### **PB-04 Add to Cart**
As a **customer**, I want to **add products to the shopping cart**, so that **I can buy them later**.

---

### **PB-05 Checkout**
As a **customer**, I want to **complete a purchase**, so that **I can receive the products I selected**.

---

### **PB-06 View Product Details**
As a **customer**, I want to **view product details**, so that **I can understand product information before buying**.
